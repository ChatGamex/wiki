# Projectiles

Projectiles are used for ranged basic attacks. Use `!projectile <id>` to equip one. Only ranged vocations (Sorcerer, Druid, Paladin) can use projectiles.

Base projectiles (Arrow, Fireball) are free. Better projectiles can be purchased permanently with `!buy projectile <id>`.

## Paladin

| ID | Projectile | Level | Cost |
|-----|-----------|-------|------|
| `arrow` | Arrow | 1 | Free |
| `icearrow` | Ice Arrow | 20 | 2,000 |
| `crystalarrow` | Crystal Arrow | 70 | 5,000 |

## Sorcerer / Druid

| ID | Projectile | Level | Cost |
|-----|-----------|-------|------|
| `fireball` | Fireball | 1 | Free |
| `icespike` | Ice Spike | 30 | 1,000 |
